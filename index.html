Step 1: Finish Creating the Repository On the screen you are looking at right now, do this: Scroll down to the bottom and click the big green "Create repository" button. (Leave everything else as the default—Public visibility is perfect). Step 2: Upload Your File Once you click that button, the screen will change. Look closely at the middle of the new page. You will see a line of text that says: "Quick setup — if you’ve done this kind of thing before" followed by some code. Look right below that code section. Find the sentence: "Get started by creating a new file or uploading an existing file." Click the blue "uploading an existing file" link. Drag and drop your index.html file into the box on the screen. Scroll down to the bottom of that page and click the green "Commit changes" button. Step 3: Turn on the Website Link Now that your file is uploaded, you just have to turn on the link: Look at the top tabs of your repository page (where it says Code, Issues, Pull Requests...). Click on Settings (the gear icon on the far right). On the left-hand sidebar, scroll down and click on Pages. Look for the Build and deployment section in the middle of the page. Under "Branch", click the dropdown menu that currently says None and change it to main (or master). Click Save. Give it about 1 to 2 minutes, refresh that same Pages screen, and GitHub will display a live link at the top of the page (e.g., https://yourusername.github.io/Imposter-game/) that you can text to your group chat!
